During peak traffic hours multiple systems experienced degradation.

Payment services saw gateway timeout issues in some regions.
In other regions payment failures were caused by network DNS latency.

Order processing systems saw delay due to Kafka consumer lag.
Some order delays were also due to database connection pool exhaustion.

Customer support also reported user errors during checkout which were not system related.

In some environments retry policies were misconfigured causing duplicate requests.

Temporary mitigations included scaling consumers, increasing timeout, and clearing stuck queues.

Permanent fixes involved query optimization, caching layer introduction, and retry policy redesign.
